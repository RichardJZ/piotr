Trekking Guide Piotr Wyszomirski

I spent many years on the world explorations. During my adventures i started discover true about life and myself. After years of explorations I decided to be trekking guide and also spiritual guide. Nowadays I feel like citizen of the whole planet. I am licensed Tourist Guide and Sudety Mountain Guide.

Most of all I like to guide individuals or small groups mainly away of main tourist routes to destinations from website and to many different places not mentioned. The main purposes of the treks are experience a great deep feelings, a beauty of joyous life, meeting different people, learning good values, improving ourselves, having some good adventures.

Expeditions are not pointed to physical comfort but the main purpose besides adventures is achieving high level of spiritual wellbeing what means building long term peace, love, hope, faith, harmony, joy and strength within. It is about deep contemplation and meditation the magic beauty of the world and coming back home fulfilled and happy. It is about freeing from materialistic comfort and addiction and achieving spiritual goals.
